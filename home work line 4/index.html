potato salad

Here’s the ingredients you’ll need for this classic potato salad:

Ingredients

  • potatoes
  • hard boiled eggs
  • yellow mustard
  • salt and fresh ground pepper
  • green onion

Preparation

  1. Add the potatoes to a large pot of cold water and bring to a boil. Reduce the heat to a lightly rolling boil over medium heat and add 1 teaspoon of salt. Cook for 20-25 minutes
  2. Add the celery and the green onions to the potato mixture. Chop 4 of the hard boiled eggs and add to the potato mixture.
  3. In a bowl, mix the Miracle Whip or mayonnaise, yellow mustard, celery seed and salt and pepper. Fold into the potato mixture and season with more salt and pepper to taste
  4. Slice the last egg into thin slices and place the slices on top of the salad
